PARC: Contrast

Apple

Apple.com

Apple uses contrast to differentiate between it's different products, utilizing a black background for one product, and a white background for another.

PARC: Alignment

KSL News

Ksl.com

Alignment is used to when aligning the images vertically. The stories are also vertically and horizontally aligned. All elements are aligned using a grid.

PARC: Proximity

Pandora

Pandora.com

Proximity is used to group the individual plans together for Pandora's music service, avoiding confusion and seeing the elements as a group that belongs together.
